About Us

SpeX is an innovative pair of smart glasses designed to assist individuals with visual impairments. By leveraging advanced AI and sensor technology, speX offers real-time features such as object recognition, text reading, and face identification, enabling users to navigate their surroundings with ease. Its sleek and lightweight design ensures comfort throughout the day while providing users with a seamless experience.

Whether it's reading signs, identifying obstacles, or recognizing familiar faces, speX is built to provide immediate, accurate, and user-friendly support. The glasses are lightweight, stylish, and designed to provide all-day comfort without compromising on performance.
